About me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at Northeastern University. My Ph.D. research concentrates on designing and applying computational tools based on electronic structure theory and machine learning to understand and design heterogeneous catalysts. My current research focuses on the fundamental chemistry in lithium-mediated nitrogen reduction reaction (NRR).
