About me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Northeastern University. My Ph.D. research concentrates on designing and applying computational tools based on quantum mechanics and machine learning to understand and design heterogeneous catalysts. My current research focuses on designing catalysts for nitrogen redcution reaction (NRR).
